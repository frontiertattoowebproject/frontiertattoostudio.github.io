RI - TATTOO ARTIST

Hey! I’m Ri and I am a neurodivergent LGBTQ+ artist who has been tattooing since early 2021. My favourite tattoo styles are anything anime/video game/nerdy/horror related, florals/nature and fineline styles both in black & white and colour.

Still, I dabble in a little of everything as I try to find my niche in tattooing. My goal is to provide a positive, fun and safe experience for all my clients. All (18+) bodies will always be welcome in my chair. My pronouns are She/Her. Outside of tattooing, I am a parent to four animals (three cats & a dog) with my partner and I love everything pink, pastel and horror-themed. I love playing video games with some of my favorites being Harvest Moon, Resident Evil & Animal Crossing.

I am a Leo sun, Leo moon & an Aquarius rising. Art has always been an important part of my life, and being able to pursue a career that is so artistic has been a dream come true for me. I look forward to meeting new clients & value building great client relationships along the way.
